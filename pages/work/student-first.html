The Brief

A brand lift, modern website, and strategic messaging empowers Student First to confidently navigate the challenges of the SIS market.

Challenge

Confronted with a higher education landscape known for its complexity and resistance to change, Student First needed to engage multiple buyer personas and C-level decision-makers, each with unique concerns and priorities. These hurdles required a strategic approach, beginning with a brand lift that would convey the right look, feel, and messaging to diverse target audiences.

Solution

A transformative brand journey from the ground up.

We provided a comprehensive brand lift, infusing every aspect with strategic messaging and stunning visuals. At the heart of the solution was a modern website, meticulously designed to showcase Student First's value proposition with clarity and impact. The Brand Essentials package elevated Student First's identity, ensuring consistency and resonance across all touchpoints — empowering the team to confidently navigate the SIS market and make a compelling case for change.
