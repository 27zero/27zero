The Brief

Organizing a bold, immersive event blending Cartagena's magic with Ellucian's innovative vision for higher education — the 2024 LAC User Conference.

Challenge

Creating an unforgettable, engaging experience for 200 key EdTech leaders across Latin America and the Caribbean wasn't just about logistics — it was about creating magic. The event needed to reflect Ellucian's core values of flexibility, agility, simplicity, and composability, all while embracing the vibrant spirit of Cartagena, with language barriers, sponsor visibility, and seamless execution to consider.

Solution

The Hotel Estelar Cartagena Bocagrande, transformed.

We blended Cartagena's magical realism with Ellucian's vision through strategic design, vibrant graphics, and impeccable attention to detail. A state-of-the-art stage, sponsor-focused spaces, and immersive experiences — including a white party at Andrés Carne de Res and a catamaran ride around Cartagena Bay — ensured attendees felt inspired and engaged. Simultaneous translation enabled seamless communication across languages.

The Impact

67 Institutions

Visitors from 67 institutions and organizations across 13 countries, for a total of 194 attendees.

Glowing Feedback

The Ellucian team remarked: "In 10 years of doing this, this is the first time we've really felt listened to."

New Relationships

Flawless logistics, creative design, and close collaboration generated immediate new leads and solidified strong relationships across the region.
