The Brief

Revolutionizing scholarship applications in higher education demanded a bold brand identity.

Challenge

As a new product in the scholarship application space, Scholarship Magic needed a standout brand identity. Clear, compelling messaging was necessary to communicate the value proposition to several stakeholders within the buying process, as well as the results and impact to the donor community facilitating the scholarships — all delivered swiftly within budget limitations, without compromising quality.

Solution

A vibrant, dynamic brand identity and positioning.

The new brand identity — including a brand new website — captured the essence of Scholarship Magic's innovative solution. Through targeted market research, we crafted messaging that resonated with multiple target audiences, highlighting the simplicity and effectiveness of the platform. Sales teams were equipped with cohesive, professional branded materials — a win for Sales, Product, and Marketing alike.

The Impact

Crafted

A striking brand look and feel that captivated audiences, evoking playfulness and ease simultaneously.

Developed

Messaging that resonated across audiences, making the value proposition evident for administrators, donors, and students alike.

Accelerated

Time-to-value by delivering a brand essentials kit and website in just under two months.

Implemented

Sales enablement and support tools to empower the sales team and drive conversion.
