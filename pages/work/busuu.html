The Brief

Film and produce a series of case studies with world-renowned brands across Europe.

Challenge

Communicate the key benefits of the Busuu B2B offering, building on the well-established consumer language-learning platform, using only filmed customer interviews shot in various locations across the continent.

Solution

A standardized production process for global teams.

While our team is experienced in managing demanding filming and interview schedules for Customer Spotlights, this project presented an additional challenge: establishing a process that could be replicated consistently by external video teams worldwide. The editing process was centralized using visual templates that highlighted previously unexplored elements of the Busuu brand.

The Impact

Showcased

Customer Spotlights highlighting users from world-renowned brands such as PUMA and L'Oréal.

Highlighted

Product Spotlights, showcasing the key benefits of learning languages at scale.

Featured

Industry Spotlights, emphasizing the specific benefits for industries where language learning is essential.
