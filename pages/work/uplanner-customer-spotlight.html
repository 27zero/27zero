The Brief

Produce visually stunning case studies through video and print to showcase uPlanner's capability and impact in Latin America and drive growth.

Challenge

While uPlanner had established relationships with leading universities in Latin America, their success stories remained largely undocumented. To expand brand presence regionally and globally, uPlanner sought to create a portfolio of case studies across the region — fueling pipeline, solidifying its refreshed branding, and strengthening relationships with existing clients.

Solution

Brand trust through customer voice.

We built brand trust and credibility by engaging customers to share their experience with uPlanner through video and print, creating a case study portfolio usable across varying formats and diverse campaigns to help close deal opportunities and bring in new leads.

The Impact

Produced

10+ customer spotlights across varying countries, highlighting experiences and results across the diverse portfolio offering.

Leveraged

Existing customer relationships, giving them a voice to share their institutional successes.
