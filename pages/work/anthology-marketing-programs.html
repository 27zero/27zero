The Brief

The Antología project brief was to create an inspiring marketing initiative to strengthen LatAm partnerships and meet key business goals for the coming year.

Challenge

The primary challenge was to unify multiple teams and stakeholders to visualize and understand the benefits of the project. With only four months to schedule and eleven months to execute, the project required meticulous planning and coordination to ensure timely delivery and high-quality production.

Solution

The answer: Antología.

27zero proposed Antología, a web documentary series that captures the true stories of unsung heroes in Latin American education. The project involved traveling through seven countries to conduct 56 interviews across 28 institutions, telling the stories of 15 protagonists. The series was structured into five chapters, each featuring a video summary, infographics, profiles of contributors and guests, and a companion podcast.

The Impact

Premiere

Antología successfully premiered at Anthology Together '23, showcasing a cinema-inspired stage design.

Media Presence

The project garnered six appearances in Tier-1 media, including CNN en Español, and generated 50 new opportunities in the pipeline, with nearly half already converted.

Outcome

Antología not only met but exceeded expectations, solidifying 27zero's reputation for innovative and impactful EdTech marketing solutions.
